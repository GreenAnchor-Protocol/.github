# GreenAnchor Protocol 🌿 ⚓

> **Bridging the gap between Corporate Sustainability and the Stellar Network.**

GreenAnchor is an enterprise-grade ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) orchestration layer. We provide a transparent, high-performance toolkit for tokenizing, tracking, and retiring Carbon Credits as Real-World Assets (RWAs) using the Stellar network and Soroban smart contracts.

---

## 🚀 Mission
Our goal is to eliminate "greenwashing" by providing immutable, on-chain proof of environmental impact. By leveraging Stellar's low-cost, carbon-neutral infrastructure, we empower SMEs and Enterprises to manage their carbon footprint with the same precision as their financial ledger.

## 🛠️ The GreenAnchor Stack
Our architecture is designed for scalability, security, and developer experience:

* **Frontend:** `React 19` + `Tailwind CSS` (High-performance, accessible ESG dashboards).
* **Backend:** `NestJS` (Microservices architecture for off-chain data validation and Oracle integration).
* **Blockchain:** `Stellar SDK` + `Soroban (Rust)` (For asset minting, atomic retirement, and compliance logic).
* **Standards:** Built on `SEP-24` (Interoperability) and `SEP-10` (Web Authentication).

---

## 🌟 Why Stellar?
GreenAnchor utilizes the **Stellar Network** for three primary reasons:
1.  **Asset Issuance:** Native support for regulated assets with "Clawback" and "Auth-Required" flags.
2.  **Sustainability:** Stellar’s Consensus Protocol (SCP) is energy-efficient, aligning with our ESG values.
3.  **Liquidity:** Easy integration with the Stellar Decentralized Exchange (DEX) for carbon credit trading.
